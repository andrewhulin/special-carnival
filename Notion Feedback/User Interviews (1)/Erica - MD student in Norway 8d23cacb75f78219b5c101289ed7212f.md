# Erica - MD student in Norway

Interview Date: February 25, 2026
Interviewer: Derrick Hull
Key Themes: Found via friend/family
Paid v. Organic: Organic
Primary Persona: N/A
User Description: Erica is a mid-20s 3rd year med student, was using ChatGPT and let that slip to her AI+med tech mother who told her to stop and use something safer and better like Ash. She’s been using it and loving it ever since

**Erica’s Background & Context**
• Third-year medical student in Norway
• Grew up in US, family from Scandinavia
• Mother is AI-in-medicine researcher who recommended Ash
• Previously used ChatGPT for life advice and reflection**Ash Discovery & Adoption**
• Downloaded Ash in November after mother’s recommendation
• Mother’s concerns about ChatGPT:
    ◦ Lack of regulations, especially dangerous for struggling teenagers
    ◦ Data privacy issues
• Usage pattern:
    ◦ Nov-Dec: 1-2x per week
    ◦ January: No usage (traveling with friends)
    ◦ Current: ~1x per week**Ash vs ChatGPT Comparison**
• Ash feels more like a therapist:
    ◦ Asks probing questions to encourage deeper reflection
    ◦ Curious approach that helps users figure things out
    ◦ More interactive, guided conversation style
• ChatGPT approach:
    ◦ Provides immediate analysis in paragraph form
    ◦ More directive with bulleted lists and solutions
    ◦ Less conversational, more informational**Primary Use Cases & Value**
• Main topics: Relationship navigation (friendships, parents) > school stress
• Seeking strategies and actionable next steps rather than emotional validation
• Values Ash’s ability to:
    ◦ Provide multiple perspectives on situations
    ◦ Help understand personal feelings and reactions
    ◦ Suggest conversation approaches with others
    ◦ Function as “live journal” with interactive questioning**User Experience Feedback**
• Loves insights feature - helps learn about herself and notice unrecognized patterns
• Appreciates when Ash accurately reflects back her experiences
• Insights accuracy varies but generally helpful for reflection
• No specific improvement requests - satisfied with current experience
• Enjoys being analyzed and gaining self-understanding
