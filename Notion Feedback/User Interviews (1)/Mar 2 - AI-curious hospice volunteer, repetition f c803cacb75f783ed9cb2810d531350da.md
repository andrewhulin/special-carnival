# User Experience & Frustrations

- Feels Ash is "more aimed at an audience with a slightly different set of problems than her" — specifically, it assumes she has a level of social anxiety she doesn't often feel
    - Overly careful about social interactions; asks "how did you feel about that" repeatedly
    - Told Ash multiple times she doesn't have social anxiety, but it disregarded the feedback
    - Note: A few users have noted Ash's tendency to make incorrect assumptions about their experience. Some find this useful for reflection; others find it annoying.
- Picks one topic and sticks to it across sessions
    - Likes persistence of memory — feels natural and supportive
    - However, this focus on a single topic can be frustrating when daily problems vary
- Response length issues: "barf of words" — overwhelming volume; preferences vary by time/context
- Notification timing problems: turned off notifications due to poor timing (often prompted during busy morning hours)
    - **Important:** She wasn't clear on how to adjust when notifications are sent — opportunity to make this capability clearer and easier to access

# Usage Patterns & Engagement

- Started almost daily, then every 2–3 days when busier
- Found interactions "gripping" and hard to put down
    - Felt personal obligation to complete conversations, different from other GPTs she can "ignore"
    - Often sets aside dedicated time chunks because she anticipates conversations may be lengthy
- Said Ash's repetitiveness reduced daily appeal — possible retention impact worth investigating
- More personal sense of engagement than typical AI tools
    - Ash's combination of validation + positive feedback + something to work on keeps her returning

# Improvement Suggestions

- Weekly check-ins asking about the current week's problems
- Better user feedback collection and responsiveness — she wants to take the lead in conversations and direct them more flexibly, rather than Ash leading
- Time-of-day preference settings for notifications
    - Generally, significant opportunity to improve notification experience and customization
- Praised brevity of current onboarding, but suggested Ash ask about what types of issues she needs help with (or specific diagnoses)
    - Aligns with feedback from other users: wanting to customize Ash based on their specific issues vs. Ash's personality traits
